Step 2 of 3

Activity Level

Be honest - overestimating activity is the #1 reason calorie targets miss. When in doubt, go one level lower.

Sedentary
Little or no exercise. Desk job. Mostly sitting throughout the day. Multiplier: 1.2×
Lightly Active
Light exercise 1–3 days/week. Casual walks, some gym time. Multiplier: 1.375×
Moderately Active
Structured training 3–5 days/week. This is most FIT 40 members. Multiplier: 1.55×
Very Active
Hard training 6–7 days/week or a physical job. High daily output. Multiplier: 1.725×
Super Active
Intense daily training plus a very physical job. Athlete-level output. Multiplier: 1.9×

Daily Macro Breakdown

Protein is set first at 1g per lb of bodyweight - non-negotiable for muscle retention over 40. Fat stays fixed at 30% to support hormones and joints. Carbs are your lever - they go up when you need more energy and performance, down when you're cutting. That's it. That's the system.

Fine-Tune Your Number
The formula gets you close. Your body gets the final vote. After 3 to 4 weeks of honest tracking: not seeing progress, nudge down. Energy or strength dropping, nudge up. Small moves only.
